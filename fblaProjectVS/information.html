Information

Arena Overview

  • Capacity: 3,500 spectators
  • Stevenson High School's new gymnasium
  • Home to the Stevenson Patriots teams
  • Hosts various school and community events

Amenities

  • State-of-the-art sound system
  • LED scoreboards
  • Concession stands with various options
  • Ample parking available
  • Wheelchair accessible

Directions

  • Address: 1 Patriot Way, Stevenson, IL 60069
    • Google Maps: Directions to Patriot Arena

Policies

  • No outside food or drinks allowed
  • Disruptive behavior will face consequences
  • Smoking is prohibited except in designated areas
  • All guests and their belongings are subject to security screening upon entry
  • All guests must have a valid ticket and sit in their assigned seat
